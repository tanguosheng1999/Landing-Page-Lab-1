OUR STORY

Was established in 1990 after Tan Kim Tai discovered grafting. Twenty years ago, his son, Tan Peo Chuan (popularly called Ah Di), switched this 6-acre durian farm to pesticide-free organic farming to produce healthy, high-quality durian. Our stall provide all types of local Durians such as Musang King, D24, XO, Tracka, Udang Merah and etc for retails and Durian party.
